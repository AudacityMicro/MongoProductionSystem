Robot 04 / Mill pallet transfer

Load or unload the mill

Load assumes Mongo is already holding a pallet. Unload picks the pallet from the mill and leaves it held. The mill must already be at its configured G53 load/unload position. This sends only the robot motion and does not change the schedule or Robot-held state.
